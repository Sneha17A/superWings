About Us

At Super wings education is cultivated more as the nation building excercise more than learning programs. Our goal is to make students best in all aspects of there career as well as in their life. We have started our preschool in the year of 2011 at Ambattur, then we have started our kinderkarten School at Kolathur and also Kathirvedu then we developed our education service from kids to Queens.
